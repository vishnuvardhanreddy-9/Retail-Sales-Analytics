# Retail Sales Analytics

## Business Problem

RetailHub Pvt. Ltd. wants to analyze its sales data to improve business decisions.

The company needs insights into:

- Sales Performance
- Customer Behaviour
- Product Performance
- Inventory
- Employee Performance
- Store Performance

---

## Project Objectives

1. Build a Retail Sales Database.

2. Generate realistic retail data.

3. Perform SQL analysis.

4. Build Power BI dashboards.

5. Publish project on GitHub.

---

## KPIs

- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Total Customers
- Average Order Value
- Customer Lifetime Value
- Monthly Revenue
- Inventory Turnover
- Top Products
- Top Stores
- Top Employees
